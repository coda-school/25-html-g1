Curriculum

The curriculum has been created to prepare students to become fullstack developers, mainly centered around the web.

From the fundamentals to the highest levels, the pupils will discover many facets of the world of coding, all while being accompanied by professional speakers to teach them how companies truly work.

After a few years, they will also be able to choose a specialty to learn more about what they truly enjoy and if they want to go even further, they can obtain a masters degree after five whole years.
