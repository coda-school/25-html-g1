Campus life

On campus, students have a strong sense of community and team spirit.

Many assignments are done in groups, and even oral presentations are often collaborative, helping to build a solid team mindset.

Additionally, extracurricular activities are sometimes organized by the Coda Student Office (BDE), such as the after-work event on October 24th, the day before vacation week, in partnership with the ESADD school.
