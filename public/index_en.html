Introduction to the school wiki

Welcome to the Coda School wiki. This site is designed to provide a wealth of information about the Coda School in Dijon and its campus. Here you will find various articles about the school and its campus. The school has a student office(SO) currently being set up, which will further enhance social life on campus. Presentation of Coda

Coda School is a state-recognized computer science school that trains students up to Bac+5 (Bachelor's and Master's degrees) using modern, professional teaching methods. The Dijon campus, located at Place des Nations Unies, shares premises with ESADD (School of Digital Design) and has modern facilities (lecture hall, connected rooms, ‚Äúdesign thinking‚Äù space). The school stands out for its practice-oriented approach, work-study options for all programs, and a strong awareness of ethical issues and digital sobriety. The programs specialize in FullStack Development (Bachelor's), Systems & Networks (Bachelor's), Cybersecurity (Master's), AI & Data Science (Master's), and Software Architecture/Development (Master's), with the aim of rapid professional integration thanks to numerous local partnerships.
